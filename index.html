Backpack PDX!

A tool to help Portlanders find awesome backpacking trips in the Pacific Northwest.

Ready to hit the trail? Answer a few questions and we'll tell you where to go!


You should visit Eagle Creek

Eagle Creek trail is a spectacular hike in the Columbia Gorge, about an hour east of Portland. Backpackers can choose their adventure- an easy two-day trip of four or five miles each way, or a more rugged multi-day journey of twelve miles with 1200 feet of elevation gain.

Check out the Three Sisters Wilderness

The Three Sisters Wilderness area is located just outside of Bend, Oregon, in the Central Cascades. A number of trails criss-cross the wilderness, forming several loops of various lengths, elevation changes, and levels of difficulty.

Tackle the Timberline Trail

The Timberline Trail is a beautiful route that circles Mt. Hood. It is 39 miles in length and can be completed in as little as 3 days or as long as a week, depending on how many miles you want to travel each day and your general level of fitness.
